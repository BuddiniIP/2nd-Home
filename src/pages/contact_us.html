Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about 2nd Home

How do I verify my boarding facility?

After listing your property, our verification team will contact you within 24-48 hours to schedule a physical inspection. The verification process includes checking safety features, amenities, and location accuracy.

What should I do if I have issues with my boarding owner?

Contact our student support team immediately at support@2nd Home.com or call +94 77 123 4567. We'll mediate the situation and help find a resolution within 48 hours.

How long does it take to get a response to my inquiry?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support lines directly for faster assistance.

Can I visit boarding facilities before booking?

Yes! We encourage students to visit boarding facilities before making a decision. Use our messaging system to schedule a visit directly with the property owner.

What safety measures are in place for verified boardings?

All verified boardings must pass our safety checklist which includes: fire safety equipment, secure locks, emergency exits, proper lighting, and verified ownership documentation.
